How to use Cheque Printer

First-time setup per bank
1. Pick the bank and currency.
2. Upload a scan of your blank cheque (Cheque Background section).
3. Turn on Edit Mode. Drag fields to align with your scan, drag the corner handle to resize.
4. Save Layout. The bank remembers its layout, background, and font sizes.
5. Use Print Preview to verify before printing.
Printing
1. Load a blank cheque or plain paper.
2. Background is preview-only by default — toggle on for plain paper.
3. In the print dialog: paper size 178×84mm, margins None, headers/footers off.
Bilingual amounts
For MVR, the Dhivehi amount is generated automatically (Sofwath/NumberToThaana vocabulary). Toggle Print Dhivehi line to include in output.
Undated cheques
Toggle Leave undated to skip the date. Choose to leave blank, print a placeholder, or write custom text.
Payees
Save frequent payees once. Type in the Payee field to see suggestions, or use the ⊕ button. CSV import/export available.
